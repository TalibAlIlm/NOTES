What is Talib al-ʿIlm?

A structured, print-ready digital notebook for Islamic studies. Take notes directly on A4 pages with four color-coded block types — Āyah (Quranic verses), Ḥadīth (prophetic narrations), Athar/Sharḥ (scholar commentary), and personal Notes.

Every document is formatted for printing with authentic geometric ornaments and clean Islamic typography.

🔄
Spaced Repetition — Study Smarter

The app has a built-in Spaced Repetition System (SRS) based on the SM-2 algorithm — the same science used by Anki and medical students worldwide.

After reading your notes, rate how well you retained the material — Forgot, Hard, Good, or Easy. The app calculates when to show you the document again: longer gaps for strong recall, shorter for weak recall.

Documents due for review are highlighted in the library. Over time, material moves into long-term memory with minimal repetition.

⚠
Important — Your Data & Storage

Your notes are saved in this browser's local storage only.

  • If you clear your browser data or cookies, all notes will be deleted.
  • Use the Export Backup button regularly to save a JSON file of all your documents.
  • To restore, use Import Backup — your notes will be merged back in.
  • The app and your notes are tied to this specific browser on this device.
You can revisit this guide anytime from the library footer.
